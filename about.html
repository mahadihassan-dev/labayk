About Labayk

Labayk is the UK's first and only same-day nationwide Halal
meat delivery service.

We pride ourselves in providing you the freshest 100% HMC
certified Halal meat straight to your door at a time convenient
for you.

Simply order online before 3 PM and receive your fresh Halal
meat and groceries between 6-9 PM on the same day.

Avoid the hassle of travelling, parking and bad weather. At
Labayk we deliver direct to your doorstep.

And we are cheaper too.

At Labayk, we will never be beaten on price.

If you find a similar product cheaper elsewhere*, please let us
know and we will credit the difference to your next order.

*Proof including full details of date/time/product/location/delivery will be
required. This guarantee is only applicable to normally priced items and
specifically excludes sales or special offers.
